About Us

WHO WE ARE

Forteplus Projects & Services Limited is a fully indigenous Construction Company that specializes in building and engineering construction. The company was incorporated in 2007 with the aim to deliver top quality projects on time and within budget.

With over two decades of experience in the construction industry, we have built our reputation on delivering innovative construction techniques and unparalleled excellence. Our commitment to client satisfaction, integrity, innovation, teamwork, safety, and community service forms the foundation of our business philosophy.
