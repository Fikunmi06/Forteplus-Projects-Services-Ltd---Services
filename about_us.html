WHO WE ARE

Forteplus Projects & Services Limited is a fully indigenous construction company specializing in building and engineering construction. Incorporated in 2007, we deliver top‑quality projects on time and within budget through disciplined planning, rigorous safety, and a relentless focus on client outcomes.

Drawing on over two decades of industry experience, we combine innovative methods with proven project controls to execute complex works for public and private clients across Nigeria. Our culture is grounded in client satisfaction, integrity, innovation, teamwork, safety, and community service.

What We Do

We provide end‑to‑end delivery across the building lifecycle—from concept to handover:

  • Design: Architectural and engineering design that turns vision into buildable, compliant, and efficient solutions.
  • Construction: Civil and building works executed with quality materials, skilled trades, and robust HSE management.
  • Project/Construction Management: Integrated planning, cost/schedule control, and stakeholder coordination for predictable outcomes.
  • Cost Management: Cost planning, estimating, value engineering, and commercial administration to maximize value.

Our teams align programme, budget, and quality targets while proactively managing risk and change to keep your project on track.

Our Mission

To build enduring spaces that enable people and businesses to thrive—delivered safely, sustainably, and with integrity.

We pursue this mission by prioritizing client satisfaction, investing in our people, embracing innovation, and holding ourselves to the highest standards of quality and safety on every project.
